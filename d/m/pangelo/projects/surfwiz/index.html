Surfwiz

This project gathers information about DIY surfboard fabrication tools and techniques, in order to help make surfboard design and customization more approachable for beginners.

Possible outcomes:

  • a user-focused parametric surfboard design tool
  • a lower cost fabrication process assisted by digital fabrication techniques that uses environmentally friendly materials
  • new customization options (like using embedded lights and sensors)

Materials

  • EPS foam, traditional material, can be recycled
  • Aerogel, possible alternative to foam core, incredibly expensive
  • Mushroom Mycelium, 100% organic and biodegradable, but needs to be grown rather than shaped.
  • Natural fiber cloth (hemp, sisal, flax), cheaper, lighter and more environmentally friendly than glass cloth.
  • Cork
  • Pine resin and beeswax as structural adhesive
  • Bio-resins to replace traditional epoxy resins

    • Ecopoxy
